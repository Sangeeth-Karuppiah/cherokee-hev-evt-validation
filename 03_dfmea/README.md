# DFMEA

This folder contains Design Failure Mode & Effects Analysis (DFMEA) templates and risk prioritization guides.

## Contents

- **dfmea_structure_example.xlsx** - DFMEA template (AIAG/VDA-compliant, coming soon)
- **risk_prioritization_guide.md** - Approach to RPN calculation and mitigation tracking (coming soon)

## Purpose

DFMEA is a structured approach to identify potential failure modes, assess their impact, and prioritize mitigation actions for hybrid powertrain subsystems.
